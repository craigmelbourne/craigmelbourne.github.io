Modals

Considerations

  • Models consits of three elements
    • Title
    • Content
    • Close button
  • Allow for scrolling within the content area if content is longer that available screen height
  • Can be fluid (e.g. map view) or fixed width (e.g. date interstitial) depending on content/functionality
  • Go full width on small screen devices to maximise screen real estate
