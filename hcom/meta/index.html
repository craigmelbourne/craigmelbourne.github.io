States to consider

  • Introduce (Visibility level, placement on page, customer segmentation)
  • Explain (What is this? What are the rules? Why are you doing it?)
  • Prove (Convince me that you've given me the best price')
  • Redeem (Reduce purchase friction)

Some of the rules

  • Only applies to a certain number of hotels
  • Only applies to cheapest available room for selected hotels
  • Only applies to certain search criteria (length of stay, number of travellers etc...)
  • Compares on cheapest available room price (not guaranteed to room match)
  • Will only be redeemable is price difference is a specific amount (€10)
  • Needs the user to trigger an action to redeem coupon
