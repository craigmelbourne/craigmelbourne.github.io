Top 10 islands in the world

Share

Tweet

From barefoot escapes straight out of Robinson Crusoe to rocky isles lashed by the Atlantic, this guide to the top 10 islands in the world will help you choose the right hotel.

Mamanuca Islands, Fiji

Strung like pearls across the Pacific, the Mamanuca Islands are the ultimate desert-island escape, as seen in the film Cast Away. Sugar-white beaches, swaying coconut palms and coral reefs swirling with fish live up to the brochure’ promise.

Hotels in Mamanuca Islands

Easter Island

Easter Island, or Rapa Nui as locals call it, is an enigma of an island. Marooned thousands of miles from anywhere, this near-treeless island is famous for its mysterious maoi, monolithic ancestor statues that have stood in silent testimony to the ebb and flow of the Pacific for some 1,000 years.

Hotels in Easter Island

Ko Phi Phi, Thailand

Ko Phi Phi’s limestone outcrops rearing above the turquoise Andaman Sea, white sand bays and tangled jungle interior were captured in the movie The Beach. Slip into the island rhythm on a longtail boat ride, swaying in a hammock in front of your waterside hut or snorkelling in crystal waters.

Hotels in Ko Phi Phi

Santorini

Santorini is picture postcard Greece with its chalk-white houses and blue-domed churches spilling down sheer lava cliffs to the glittering Aegean Sea. The volcanic island is best appreciated from a bougainvillea-clad terrace, enjoying a cold ouzo, hearing bouzouki music and watching the sun go down.

Hotels in Santorini

Orkney Islands

Puffins perch on swell-battered cliffs and grey seals bask on the rocks of the Orkney Islands off the northern tip of Scotland. The islands’ rugged coastline fringes countryside in myriad shades of green that is flecked with lonesome stone cottages and mystical prehistoric standing stones.

Hotels in Orkney Islands

Praslin, Seychelles

The azure Indian Ocean laps the ivory sands and gigantic granite boulders of Praslin in the Seychelles, one of the top 10 islands in the world. A favourite among honeymooners for romantic hotels and dreamy views, the island’s secluded beaches skirt a jungly interior bristling with coco de mer palms.

Hotels in Praslin Island

Maui, Hawaii

Sculpted into rugged glory by volcanic eruptions, Maui is a shape-shifting Hawaiian island. One minute you’re hiking jungle trails to misty waterfalls, the next you’re spotting humpback whales or stretching out on a crescent bay. Take in the island’s finest scenery by negotiating the hairpins of the Hana Highway.

Hotels in Maui

Fraser Island, Australia

Many islands boast about never-ending golden beaches, but few can compete with those of Fraser Island. This UNESCO-listed island is the world’s biggest sand bar, famed for its coloured sand cliffs, towering dunes and 75-mile beach. Beyond the beach, a 4WD takes you to mangroves, freshwater lakes and lush rainforest.

Hotels in Fraser Island

Capri

A boat speeds you across from Italy’s Amalfi Coast to the chic island of Capri in the Bay of Naples. Cave-riddled cliffs plunge down to deep blue water and tiny coves on the coast, while the interior’s fragrant lemon groves are studded with villas. Take the lead of locals with an evening passeggiata (stroll) to a trattoria serving Mediterranean-fresh fish.

Hotels in Capri
