/ CASE STUDIES

[Disclaimer: I've spent the last 7 years working for Hotels.com so these case studies are based on my time there]

Hotels.com / Product optimisation

Optimising the product display to help travellers select the right property

To hit their growth targets Hotels.com invested heavily in increasing the volume and diversity of accommodation options in their product. I worked on the creation of an experience strategy for a more scalable and personalised property details experience.

Hotels.com / Experience Strategy

Adapting product experience to address changes in demand post-Covid

The pandemic brought a change in travel demand and consumer needs requiring the team to quickly adapt their short - mid term strategy. I was asked to help synthesise the research, organise the team around a program of work and lead on some product discovery .

Hotels.com / Product optimisation

Evolving the map experience to help travellers find the right location to stay in

Optimising each stage in the customer journey is key to hitting business objectives and creating a best in class customer experience. On this project I worked on the creation of a product experience backlog to make a step change improvement to map view on the Hotels.com website and app.
