Craig Melbourne

User Experience Design and Leadership

A seasoned design leader with entrepreneurial energy, who thrives in highly complex, ambiguous environments. By balancing design practices, business acumen and influence, I guide teams and projects to consistently produce best in class experiences that deliver business objectives.

/ Skills

  • Shaping Experience strategy - translating corporate and product strategy into an inspiring experience North Star vision and collaborating on defining the objectives, principles and operating plan to get there.
  • Leading design organisations - experience with all aspects of leading and scaling a culturally and cognitively diverse team of ICs and managers including attracting and hiring talent, coaching and mentoring, career development paths, adapting org design and establishing design as a key organisational strategic asset.
  • Design discovery - identifying and evaluating potential solutions through synthesis of data and research, competitor analysis, facilitation of idea generation, prototype creation and testing
  • Design delivery - drawing on expertise in experience architecture and interface design to create intuitive and frictionless user flows an interfaces across mobile (iOS and Android), desktop and TV devices.
  • Impactful design operations - embedding tools, processes and frameworks that empower designers (and non-designers) to play a key role in efficiently delivering experiences that meet and exceed customer needs.
  • Future opportunity discovery - through building a deep understanding of customer motivations, goals and needs; and observing emerging industry and technology trends I work with teams to explore new product and service offerings.
